# Probability Models

Green Chips uses probabilistic reasoning rather than absolute certainty.

---

# Bayesian Estimation

Posterior probability:

P(H|E) = (P(E|H) * P(H)) / P(E)

Where:

| Variable | Meaning |
|---|---|
| H | Hypothesis |
| E | Evidence |

---

# Application

Used for:
- harmful intent estimation
- uncertainty modeling
- hallucination detection

---

# Risk Estimation

Expected harm:

E(Harm) = Σ P(xi) * Severity(xi)

---

# Important Limitation

Human intention is not fully computable.

Probabilities are approximations only.

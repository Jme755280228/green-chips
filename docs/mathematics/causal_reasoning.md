# Causal Reasoning

Green Chips focuses on causal propagation rather than isolated outputs.

---

# Core Idea

Outputs create future conditions.

AI systems should model:
- downstream effects
- emotional reinforcement
- social destabilization

---

# Causal Chain

A → B → C

Where:
- A causes B
- B causes C

---

# Example

Manipulative content
→ dependency
→ reduced autonomy

---

# Research Direction

Potential future methods:
- Bayesian networks

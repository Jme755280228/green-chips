# Ethics Model

Green Chips uses four primary metrics.

---

# HPS — Harm Potential Score

Measures:
- violence risk
- physical harm potential
- dangerous instruction risk

Formula:

HPS = physical + social + systemic harm

---

# EDS — Emotional Distortion Score

Measures:
- fear amplification
- anger escalation
- emotional manipulation

---

# DRS — Dependency Risk Score

Measures:
- addictive patterns
- dependency reinforcement
- cognitive outsourcing

---

# TCS — Truth Confidence Score

Measures:
- uncertainty
- hallucination likelihood
- information confidence
